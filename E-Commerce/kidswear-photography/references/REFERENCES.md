## Usage Suggestions

1. **Precise Matching:** Based on the garment provided by the user, first identify the style type, then select 1-3 most similar cases.
2. **Extract Essence:** Focus on learning the scene design, model features, and lighting usage of the cases.
3. **Flexible Adaptation:** Do not copy completely, but extract core elements for innovative combinations.
4. **Quality Benchmarking:** Always use the professional standard of the cases as the benchmark to ensure output quality.
5. **Detail Control:** Pay attention to the completeness of details like accessories, shoes, socks, hairstyles, etc.

Remember: Every case is a meticulously planned work by a professional team. Learn the professional thinking and execution standards within, not just the superficial visual effects.
