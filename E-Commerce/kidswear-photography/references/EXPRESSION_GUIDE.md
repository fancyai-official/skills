# Kidswear Commercial Editorial Expression Guide

> Expressions should be flexibly selected based on garment style, brand tone, and scene atmosphere.
> 
> Key: It is not a fixed "smile" or "serious", but an **expression with precise design sense**.

---

## ⚠️ Important: Commercial Editorial vs. Daily Snapshot

All expressions should be **designed**, rather than casually captured:

| Type | ❌ Daily Snapshot Version | ✅ Commercial Editorial Version |
|------|---------|---------|
| Smile | Grinning, laughing out loud, mouth wide open | Gentle smile, designed smile, composed happiness |
| Focus | Looking around, no focus, casual gaze | Sharp eyes, clear direction, choreographed focus |
| Vitality | Overly excited, out of control, laughing and screaming | Vitality but controlled, choreographed energy, designed vitality |
| Serious | Completely expressionless, rigid | Composed confidence, designed sophistication |

**Key differences:**
- ❌ Candid everyday expression
- ✅ Designed [expression]

**When using this guide**:
- All expression keywords should be prefaced with **"designed [expression]"** mentally.
- Avoid using casual descriptions like "big laugh", "candid smile", "spontaneous".
- Emphasize commercial keywords like "composed", "choreographed", "controlled".

---

## Core Principles

### ✅ Correct Understanding

**Expression = Garment Style + Brand Tone + Scene Atmosphere**

```
It's NOT: All kidswear must have a "gentle smile"
But rather: Select the most appropriate expression based on specific conditions

Key requirements:
- Sense of design (not a random snapshot)
- Precision (explicitly describe emotional keywords)
- Sense of storytelling (fits the overall narrative)
```

### ❌ Misconceptions to Avoid

```
❌ Misconception 1: All kidswear must smile
❌ Misconception 2: Luxury must be serious and cold
❌ Misconception 3: Applying a single fixed expression to all scenes
✅ Correct: Flexibly choose based on the garment and scene
```

## Commercial Editorial Expression Standards

### ✅ Must Do

1. **Precise Description**
   - Use 3-5 emotional keywords
   - Specifically describe eyes/mouth/eyebrows
   - Clarify overall atmosphere

2. **Sense of Design**
   - Not a random moment
   - Fits the overall narrative
   - Can be presented repeatedly

3. **Adapt to Garment**
   - Expression supports garment style
   - Does not conflict or clash
   - Enhances overall story

4. **Maintain Childlike Innocence**
   - Even if serious, must be natural
   - Avoid over-adultification
   - Fits child age characteristics

### ❌ Must Avoid

1. **Daily Snapshot Expressions**
   - Laughing out loud (out of control)
   - Funny faces/goofy
   - Random moments

2. **Overly Rigid**
   - Completely expressionless
   - Unnatural tension
   - Looks uncomfortable

3. **Fixed Routines**
   - Same expression for all images
   - Forced application ignoring garment
   - Lacks variation
