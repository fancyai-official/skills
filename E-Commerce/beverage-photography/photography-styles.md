# 5 Shooting Styles In-Depth

Deep analysis of 5 core styles in beverage commercial photography, based on 90+ top-tier case studies.

---

## Style 1: Dynamic Splash

### Style Characteristics

High-speed photography capturing liquid motion, emphasizing dynamism, power, and visual impact.

**Core Visual Elements**
- Graceful curves formed by liquid splash
- Crystalline texture of flying droplets
- A frozen dynamic moment
- High-contrast dramatic effect

**Color Profile**
- Pure black or deep background to highlight liquid
- Vivid color of the liquid itself
- Bright white of highlights and droplets
- Overall high contrast

**Best Products**
- Wine (red wine splash is the classic)
- Spirits (whiskey, vodka liquid flow)
- Cocktails (pouring and mixing process)
- Beer (foam splash)
- Juice (fresh, clean feel)

### Reference Case Analysis

**Case 1: Stella Rosa Red Wine Liquid Splash**
- Background: Pure black
- Subject: Wine bottle centered, surrounded by red liquid splash
- Lighting: Backlit to illuminate liquid edges with crystalline transparency
- Composition: S-curve liquid wrapping around bottle
- Visual focus: Splash shape and droplet detail

**Case 2: Cocktail Pour Moment**
- Background: Pink to purple gradient
- Subject: Transparent liquid pouring into glass
- Lighting: Soft fill light capturing liquid flow
- Detail: Cherry garnish, liquid reflection, bubble detail
- Motion: Flowing line of poured liquid

**Case 3: Mojito Splash**
- Background: Light grey simple backdrop
- Subject: Glass with green drink, lime and mint flying out
- Splash: Water flying, ice cubes suspended
- Lighting: Side light illuminating water droplets
- Freshness: Green and white contrast

### Technical Points

**Lighting Setup**
- Use backlight or side-backlight
- High-speed shutter to freeze moment (above 1/2000s)
- Fill light to illuminate splash detail
- Black background absorbs excess light

**Composition Principles**
- Product centered or following rule of thirds
- Liquid splash forms visual guiding lines
- Leave space for splash
- Maintain visual balance

**Prompt Template**
```
Commercial beverage photography, [product type] with dramatic liquid splash, 
pure black background, [liquid color] liquid splashing in dynamic S-curve motion, 
frozen droplets suspended in mid-air, backlit with rim lighting creating 
glowing edges, high contrast, crystal clear liquid texture, cinematic lighting, 
ultra sharp details, professional product photography, 8k resolution
```

**Key Keyword Groups**
- Motion: splash, splashing, dynamic motion, flowing, cascading, droplets
- Lighting: backlit, rim lighting, dramatic lighting, high contrast
- Texture: crystal clear, translucent, fluid, pristine
- Background: pure black background, dark background, deep black

## Style 2: Atmospheric Still Life

### Style Characteristics

Creates a warm, high-end lifestyle atmosphere, emphasizing emotional resonance and brand story.

**Core Visual Elements**
- Real-life scenes (bar, dining table, fireside)
- Warm ambient lighting effects
- Refined glassware
- Soft bokeh background

**Color Profile**
- Warm tones dominant (amber, golden, brown)
- Soft light-to-shadow transitions
- Low saturation for premium feel
- Gold and brown in harmony

**Best Products**
- Whiskey (classic for atmospheric shots)
- Brandy, cognac
- Premium wine
- Craft beer
- Coffee, tea

### Reference Case Analysis

**Case 1: Glenfiddich Whiskey Bar Scene**
- Scene: Warm bar interior, wooden bar counter
- Subject: Whiskey bottle and two glasses showing amber liquid
- Background: Blurred bottle rack and warm lights forming circular bokeh
- Lighting: Soft warm ambient light creating premium feel
- Mood: Luxurious, relaxed, lifestyle enjoyment

**Case 2: Robert Mondavi Wine**
- Scene: Dining table, shallow depth of field
- Subject: Wine bottle centered, label clearly visible
- Background: Blurred wine glasses and restaurant environment
- Lighting: Natural light through bottle showing liquid texture
- Mood: Elegant, refined, dining culture

**Case 3: MARTINI Home Scene**
- Scene: Cozy home interior
- Subject: MARTINI bottle and cocktail glass
- Props: Colorful tablecloth, fresh fruit decorations
- Lighting: Soft natural light, warm and comfortable
- Story: Home gathering, easy and pleasant

### Technical Points

**Lighting Setup**
- Soft natural light or simulated ambient light
- Warm color temperature (3000–4000K)
- Multi-layer lighting (key light, fill light, background light)
- Shallow depth of field to blur background (f/2.8–f/4)

**Scene Setup**
- Real or simulated environment
- Carefully selected props (glasses, wood table, decorations)
- Foreground and background elements add layers
- Bokeh light spots create atmosphere

**Composition Principles**
- Golden ratio or rule of thirds
- Slight foreground blur adds spatial depth
- Background is layered but not distracting
- Eyeline leads to product

**Prompt Template**
```
Atmospheric lifestyle beverage photography, [product type] bottle and glass on 
wooden bar counter, warm ambient lighting, soft bokeh background with blurred 
lights, shallow depth of field, golden hour glow, cozy bar atmosphere, 
professional product photography, [liquid color] liquid in glass, luxury setting, 
cinematic mood, high-end commercial shot
```

**Key Keyword Groups**
- Scene: bar setting, wooden table, cozy atmosphere, intimate setting
- Lighting: warm ambient light, golden glow, soft illumination, bokeh lights
- Mood: luxury, sophisticated, cozy, elegant, intimate
- Technical: shallow depth of field, blurred background, soft focus

## Style 3: Macro Close-up

### Style Characteristics

Extreme magnification of detail, showcasing product texture and craftsmanship with delicacy and refinement.

**Core Visual Elements**
- Ultra-sharp detail rendering
- Microscopic world of ice and bubbles
- Real texture of condensation droplets
- Texture detail of garnishes

**Color Profile**
- High color saturation
- Clean, bright color scheme
- Gradient background highlights subject
- Color layers in the details

**Best Products**
- Cocktails (rich garnishes)
- Sparkling drinks (bubble detail)
- Juice (fresh fruit)
- Beer (foam and condensation)
- Coffee (milk foam texture)

### Reference Case Analysis

**Case 1: Blood Orange Cocktail Macro**
- Angle: Top-down blood orange slice
- Detail: Orange pulp texture, bubbles, ice crystals
- Background: Blue-green gradient, fresh and clean
- Lighting: Even fill light highlighting orange texture
- Color: Orange and blue-green complementary contrast

**Case 2: Pink Cocktail Bubble Close-up**
- Angle: Side close-up shot
- Focus: Ice cube and lemon slice detail
- Background: Yellow to white soft gradient
- Detail: Bubbles, ice texture, liquid layers
- Freshness: Delicate texture of pink liquid

**Case 3: Cherry Cocktail Pour**
- Angle: Side close-up
- Motion: Liquid pour frozen moment
- Detail: Cherry garnish, bubbles, liquid flow
- Background: Pink-purple gradient
- Lighting: Transmitted light showing crystalline liquid

### Technical Points

**Shooting Setup**
- Macro lens or macro mode
- Wide aperture shallow depth of field (f/2–f/4)
- Focus on key detail
- Maximum sharpness and clarity

**Lighting Setup**
- Soft even fill light
- Backlight or side light to highlight texture
- Avoid blown-out highlights
- Transmitted light shows liquid transparency

**Detail Control**
- Ice must be clear and clean
- Bubbles naturally sized and distributed
- Fruit slices fresh and full
- Perfect liquid surface tension

**Prompt Template**
```
Macro beverage photography, extreme close-up of [product type], crystal clear 
ice cubes with pristine details, [garnish] garnish, tiny bubbles and 
condensation droplets, [gradient color] gradient background, soft diffused lighting, 
shallow depth of field, ultra sharp focus on details, fresh and vibrant colors, 
commercial product photography, 8k ultra high resolution
```

**Key Keyword Groups**
- Detail: macro, close-up, extreme detail, ultra sharp, pristine
- Texture: ice cubes, bubbles, condensation, droplets, crystalline
- Freshness: fresh, vibrant, crisp, refreshing, clean
- Background: gradient background, soft background, blurred backdrop

## Style 4: Lifestyle Narrative

### Style Characteristics

Embedded in real life, telling brand stories, emphasizing situational immersion and lifestyle.

**Core Visual Elements**
- Outdoor or real-life scenes
- Lifestyle props and elements
- Natural lighting effects
- Storytelling and situational feel

**Color Profile**
- Natural and authentic colors
- Environmental tones in harmony
- Clear sense of season
- Medium-to-high saturation

**Best Products**
- Beer (outdoor, gatherings)
- Soft drinks (sports, outdoor)
- Juice (breakfast, health)
- Cocktails (parties, social)
- Ready-to-drink coffee (urban lifestyle)

### Reference Case Analysis

**Case 1: Tecate Beer Outdoor Ice Bucket**
- Scene: Beach or rocky outdoor setting
- Subject: Beer cans in ice bucket surrounded by ice
- Lighting: Natural daylight showing real texture
- Mood: Cool, outdoor, summer
- Lifestyle: Outdoor gathering, casual relaxation

**Case 2: Tecate Beer Pool Party**
- Scene: Blue pool tabletop, vivid colors
- Subject: Beer cans, beer glasses, Mexican food
- Props: Lime, chips, salsa
- Color: Blue and yellow striking contrast
- Mood: Summer, party, joyful

**Case 3: Evan Williams Whiskey Outdoor Scene**
- Scene: Outdoor checkered tablecloth, natural light
- Subject: Whiskey bottle and mason jar cocktails
- Props: Fresh peaches, mint, lemon
- Mood: Relaxed, summer, homey
- Story: Outdoor gathering, handcrafted cocktails

### Technical Points

**Scene Selection**
- Real or highly faithful life scenes
- Environment matches product positioning
- Props are rich but not cluttered
- Clear sense of season and time

**Lighting Setup**
- Natural light or simulated natural light
- Avoid overly artificial lighting
- Shadows and light feel real and natural
- Color temperature fits the environment

**Composition Principles**
- Product is visual focus but blends into scene
- Props add storytelling
- Composition is natural, not forced
- Negative space and breathing room

**Prompt Template**
```
Lifestyle beverage photography, [product type] in [scene description], natural outdoor 
lighting, vibrant colors, [prop list], casual setting, summer atmosphere, 
[color description] color palette, realistic scene, storytelling composition, 
commercial advertising photography, lifestyle marketing shot
```

**Key Keyword Groups**
- Scene: outdoor, beach, poolside, picnic, party, gathering
- Mood: casual, summer, refreshing, fun, social, festive
- Lighting: natural light, sunlight, bright daylight, outdoor lighting
- Props: ice bucket, food props, fruits, decorative elements

## Style 5: Creative Concept

### Style Characteristics

Breaking conventions, artistic expression, emphasizing visual innovation and concept communication.

**Core Visual Elements**
- Geometric and abstract elements
- Mirror reflections and transparent materials
- Bold color combinations
- Surreal visual effects

**Color Profile**
- High-saturation solid color backgrounds
- Gradient colors used broadly
- Clashing and contrasting colors
- Color as concept itself

**Best Products**
- All product types
- Especially suited for youth brands
- New product launches
- Conceptual marketing campaigns

### Reference Case Analysis

**Case 1: BRAE Geometric Metal Scene**
- Concept: Modern geometric aesthetics
- Elements: Gold geometric metal framework, purple background
- Subject: Rose gold can and crystal cocktail glass
- Material: Metal, glass, mirror reflection
- Visual: Fashionable, luxurious, modern

**Case 2: Orange Wheat Whiskey**
- Concept: Natural ingredient story
- Elements: Water ripple reflection, wheat decoration, orange slice
- Background: Pink-purple gradient
- Color: Warm tones in harmony
- Visual: Natural, warm, handcrafted feel

**Case 3: Casa Azul Tequila Blue Geometric**
- Concept: Brand color visualization
- Elements: Blue geometric box matrix
- Subject: Transparent bottle placed in geometric space
- Color: Pure blue dominant
- Visual: Extremely high brand recognition

**Case 4: Color Gradient Cocktail Series**
- Concept: Color = product
- Elements: Solid gradient backgrounds
- Subject: Drink color echoes background
- Props: Fresh fruit, flowers, decorations
- Visual: Fresh, youthful, social media friendly

### Technical Points

**Creative Directions**
- Geometric abstraction (cubes, spheres, lines)
- Mirror world (reflection, symmetry, repetition)
- Color concept (monochrome, gradient, color clash)
- Floating effect (product levitating, suspended props)
- Material contrast (metal, glass, fabric, organic)

**Visual Construction**
- Break conventional composition
- Strong color contrast
- Clear material contrast
- Defined visual focus, but innovative approach

**Execution**
- Color pairing is bold but harmonious
- Creative elements serve the product
- Maintain commercial photography quality
- Balance artistry and commerciality

**Prompt Template**
```
Creative concept beverage photography, [product type] with [creative elements], 
[color description] gradient background, geometric [shape] elements, [material] materials, 
modern minimalist aesthetic, artistic composition, bold color palette, 
floating/suspended arrangement, high-end commercial photography, 
visually striking, innovative design
```

**Key Keyword Groups**
- Creative: creative, artistic, conceptual, innovative, avant-garde
- Geometric: geometric shapes, cubes, spheres, linear, structured
- Material: metallic, glass, mirror reflection, acrylic, chrome
- Color: gradient, vibrant, bold colors, color blocking, monochromatic
- Effect: floating, suspended, surreal, minimal, clean

## Style Selection Guide

Choose style combinations based on product and marketing goal:

**Seeking visual impact** → Dynamic Splash + Creative Concept
**Emphasizing brand story** → Still Life Atmosphere + Lifestyle Narrative
**Highlighting product detail** → Macro Close-up + Dynamic Splash
**Youth / social media** → Creative Concept + Lifestyle Narrative
**Premium luxury positioning** → Still Life Atmosphere + Macro Close-up

Each style has its own visual language and use case. Flexible application creates rich and diverse commercial photography.
