# FancyAI E-Commerce Skills: Product Asset Creation

Welcome to the **FancyAI E-Commerce Skills** set. This collection is purpose-built to solve one of the most persistent challenges in modern retail: **producing high-volume, on-brand, and conversion-driven product visuals at scale** — across both still photography and motion content.

## Why We Built This

Created by the [FancyAI Team](https://fancyai.com), this skill set is a direct response to real-world friction we observed while working alongside e-commerce and creative teams at global brands.

Across **1000+ brand collaborations** — from sportswear (Adidas, Skechers, HOKA) to luxury (LVMH, Burberry) and consumer tech (Samsung, Microsoft) — four core pain points emerged consistently:

**1. Insatiable demand for asset variety** — One product needs white-background, lifestyle, model, detail, packshot, social crop, and video. Each requires different lighting, composition, and staging.

**2. Every product needs multiple contexts** — A shoe belongs on a trail, a street, and in a gym. Different categories demand different visual worlds. Each has its own vocabulary of scenes, lighting, and styling.

**3. Seasonal and campaign-driven refresh cycles** — Summer, winter, holidays, and product drops all demand fresh assets — often on impossible timelines.

**4. The hidden cost of every photoshoot** — Weeks of planning, shooting, and post-production. Multiply by hundreds of SKUs across seasons. The time, money, and creative energy add up fast.

**We built these skills to change that.** Instead of reinventing the visual strategy every time, these skills encode the planning, the context, and the creative expertise — so you can generate premium product assets in hours, not weeks.

## What This Set Solves

This folder contains product-category-specific skills that teach AI how to think like **professional e-commerce visual creators** — including both photography and video/motion experts. Each skill provides:

- **Consistent output structures** for a given product category (stills + motion)
- **Industry-standard terminology** for both photography (e.g., "hero angle," "detail shot," "lifestyle crop") and video (e.g., "product reveal," "camera sweep," "hero shot duration," "transition type")
- **Styling and background guidance** tailored to the product's context (kitchen for appliances, studio for footwear, natural light for kidswear)
- **Motion design specifications** for product animations — camera movement, pacing, dynamic reveal sequences, and social-first aspect ratios
- **Reusable prompt templates** that reduce iteration time from hours to seconds, whether you're producing a single image or a 15-second product loop

## How These Skills Are Structured

Each skill in this folder is self-contained and follows a consistent pattern:

| Skill Folder | Product Category | Key Focus (Photo + Video) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `beverage-photography` | Drinks, bottles, cans, glasses | Lighting for liquids, condensation, pouring action; motion for bubbles, pouring, and condensation formation |
| `beauty-photography` | Skincare, makeup, cosmetics | Soft lighting, reflection control, texture emphasis; video for texture application, before/after reveals |
| `kidswear-photography` | Children's apparel | Natural poses, playful context, fabric softness; video for movement, twirls, and lifestyle moments |
| `footwear-campaign` | Sneakers, shoes, boots | Hero angles, on-foot vs. product-only, material emphasis; video for 360° rotations, sole flex, and walking shots |
| `small-appliance-photography` | Coffee machines, blenders, vacuums | Tech aesthetics, glossy surface management, kitchen/lifestyle integration; video for product operation sequences |

Each skill includes a `SKILL.md` file with:
- **Role & objective** for the AI (photo and video contexts)
- **Step‑by‑step workflow** from brief to final output
- **Prompt templates** for lighting, composition, styling, and motion design
- **Industry tips** drawn from real production case studies

## Who This Is For

- **E‑commerce managers** who need consistent product imagery and video at scale
- **SMB owners** running their own online stores — who need premium product visuals without agency budgets or production teams
- **Creative producers** briefing freelance or in-house photo/video teams
- **Brand marketers** adapting assets for different markets or platforms (Instagram, TikTok, website, Amazon)
- **AI workflow developers** building automated visual pipelines for stills and motion

## Get Started

1. Choose the product category that matches your asset type
2. Copy the skill folder into your AI assistant's skills directory (e.g., `~/.claude/skills/`)
3. In your AI conversation, reference the skill by name and describe your specific product
4. Specify whether you need **photo** (still), **video** (motion), or both
5. Iterate using the provided templates — adjust lighting, composition, pacing, or camera movement as needed
