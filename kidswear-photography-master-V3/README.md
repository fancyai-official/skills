## Quality Standards & Tier System

### 3-Tier Quality Standards

This skill establishes a complete 3-tier quality standard system, detailed in [QUALITY_INSPECTION_CHECKLIST.md](QUALITY_INSPECTION_CHECKLIST.md):

**L1 - Basic Usable Tier (10 requirements must be met)**
- Accurate garment detail preservation
- Basic scene matching
- Natural model expression
- Reasonable basic composition and lighting
- No obvious AI anomalies

**L2 - Commercial Usable Tier (15 requirements, recommended ≥80% met)**
- Realism: Natural skin/hair texture, no plastic feel
- Professionalism: Natural depth of field blur, textured color tone
- Design sense: Scene props have a sense of design
- Can be used for actual e-commerce display

**L3 - Photographer Tier (20 requirements, target ≥60% met)**
- Close to real photos: Hard to recognize as AI at first glance
- Lighting has storytelling: Professional lighting design
- Strong visual impact: Outstanding composition aesthetics
- Emotional expression is in place: Storytelling and infectiousness
- Meets real photographer delivery standards

### Core Optimization Strategy - Anti-AI Realism

**Problem:** Traditional AI generation pursues "perfection", resulting in a plastic feel, wig feel, and being overly clean.

**Solution:** Counteract the AI generation tendency by "simulating real imperfection".

- Explicitly describe skin pores, blemishes
- Emphasize stray hair, natural messiness
- Add scene wear and tear, real traces
- Simulate real camera "defects" (chromatic aberration, vignette, grain)
- A large number of "NOT perfect/artificial" negative descriptions

**6-Layer Prompt Structure:**
1. Layer 0: Anti-AI realism (Core innovation)
2. Layer 1: Camera technical specs
3. Layer 2: Lighting design
4. Layer 3: Subject description
5. Layer 4: Composition angle
6. Layer 5: Color texture

Detailed guide: [ANTI_AI_PROMPTING.md](ANTI_AI_PROMPTING.md)

## Known Limitations & Coping Strategies

### Inherent limitations of nano-banana model

**1. Model training data leans towards perfection**
- Limitation: Difficult to generate "imperfect" realism
- Coping: Counteract with a large number of reverse "NOT" descriptions

**2. Limited understanding of real camera characteristics**
- Limitation: May not perfectly simulate Fujifilm color science
- Coping: Describe camera "defect" features in detail for guidance

**3. Insufficient control over complex scenes**
- Limitation: Complex props like luxury cars may deform
- Coping: Simplify scene descriptions, focus on core elements

**4. Gap with real photographers**
- Current state: Excellent results can reach L2 commercial usable tier, some reach L3 photographer tier
- Goal: Continuously optimize to approach real photographer level
- Suggestion: Accept inherent limitations of AI generation, consider post-processing when necessary

### Quality Improvement Path

**Short-term visible:**
- Plastic feel is significantly reduced
- Skin/hair is more natural
- Scene matching degree is improved

**Mid-term optimization:**
- Overall realism approaches commercial usable tier (L2)
- Can be used for actual e-commerce display
- Zooming in to check reveals no obvious fakeness

**Long-term goal:**

- Hard for ordinary people to recognize as AI-generated at first glance
