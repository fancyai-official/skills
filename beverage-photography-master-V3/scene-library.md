## Dynamic Splash Scenes (7)

### DS-01: Classic Red Wine Splash
**Best for**: Wine, red wine, rosé
**Background**: Pure black
**Subject**: Bottle centered, red liquid in S-curve splash surrounding it
**Lighting**: Strong backlight, glowing liquid edges
**Props**: No extra props needed
**Color**: Deep red liquid + pure black background
**Mood**: Dramatic, luxurious, dynamic

**Core Prompt**
```
Red wine bottle with dramatic crimson liquid splash in S-curve motion, 
pure black background, backlit with rim lighting, frozen droplets, 
high contrast, cinematic
```

- Winter → SA-02, SA-04, SA-07, DS-02, SA-06
