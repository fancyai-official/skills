---

## ⚠️ Usage Rules

**Different styles use different keywords** — never mix!

**Forbidden combinations**:
- ❌ "soft blurred background" + "highly saturated" (soft style does not use high saturation)
- ❌ "hard directional light" + "pastel tones" (hard light doesn't suit soft style)
- ❌ "minimalist" + "abundant props" (minimalist = no prop stacking)
- ❌ "texture clearly visible" + "naturally blurred" (architectural style background is NOT blurred)

**Correct combinations**:
- ✅ Soft Natural: soft blurred + pastel tones + gentle lighting + delicate props
- ✅ High-Saturation Architectural: texture clearly visible + highly saturated + hard directional light + geometric props
- ✅ Dramatic Atmospheric: dramatic lighting + deep dark tones + volumetric smoke + minimal props

## 🔧 Universal Technical Keywords (All Styles)

**Product Detail**:
- natural condensation, micro water droplets
- realistic water beads on can/bottle surface
- product label clearly readable
- authentic product texture

**Macro Anti-Distortion Guide** ⚠️:
- ✅ CORRECT: "Macro focus on condensation droplets on bottle surface, product clearly visible in background"
- ✅ CORRECT: "Extreme close-up of bubbles in glass, [Product] bottle sharp and recognizable behind"
- ✅ CORRECT: "Macro detail of ice crystals, product label readable in mid-ground"
- ❌ WRONG: "Extreme close-up of entire bottle" (causes distortion)
- ❌ WRONG: "Macro of bottle from very close" (product warping)
- **Core principle**: macro captures detail (droplets, bubbles, texture); product appears fully in mid-ground or background, sharp and undistorted

**Dynamic Splash Physics Guide** ⚠️:
- ✅ CORRECT: "Hand elegantly holding bottle, pouring liquid into glass, gentle splash" (full bottle pour must show hand)
- ✅ CORRECT: "Close-up of bottle neck pouring liquid, hand implied beyond frame" (partial pour — hand not required)
- ✅ CORRECT: "Liquid splash frozen mid-air, ice cubes falling, [Product] bottle placed on surface" (splash close-up, product static)
- ❌ WRONG: "Full bottle floating in air, pouring liquid" (full bottle suspended mid-air — physically impossible)
- ❌ WRONG: "Bottle tilted pouring without support" (unsupported tilt — not plausible)
