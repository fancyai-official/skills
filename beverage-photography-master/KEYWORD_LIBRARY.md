# Prompt 关键词库

> 按需参考：根据选定的美学风格查找对应关键词组，避免混用不同风格的词汇。

---

## ⚠️ 使用原则

**不同风格使用不同关键词**，避免混用！

**避免组合**：
- ❌ "soft blurred background" + "highly saturated"（柔和风格不用高饱和）
- ❌ "hard directional light" + "pastel tones"（硬光不适合柔和风格）
- ❌ "minimalist" + "abundant props"（极简不堆道具）
- ❌ "texture clearly visible" + "naturally blurred"（建筑风格背景不虚化）

**正确组合**：
- ✅ 柔和自然：soft blurred + pastel tones + gentle lighting + delicate props
- ✅ 高饱和建筑：texture clearly visible + highly saturated + hard directional light + geometric props
- ✅ 戏剧氛围：dramatic lighting + deep dark tones + volumetric smoke + minimal props

## 🔧 通用技术关键词（所有风格适用）

**产品细节**：
- natural condensation, micro water droplets
- realistic water beads on can/bottle surface
- product label clearly readable
- authentic product texture

**微距拍摄防变形指南** ⚠️：
- ✅ CORRECT: "Macro focus on condensation droplets on bottle surface, product clearly visible in background"
- ✅ CORRECT: "Extreme close-up of bubbles in glass, [Product] bottle sharp and recognizable behind"
- ✅ CORRECT: "Macro detail of ice crystals, product label readable in mid-ground"
- ❌ WRONG: "Extreme close-up of entire bottle" (causes distortion)
- ❌ WRONG: "Macro of bottle from very close" (product warping)
- **核心原则**: 微距拍摄细节（水珠、气泡、纹理），产品完整呈现于背景或中景，保持清晰无变形

**动态飞溅物理合理性指南** ⚠️：
- ✅ CORRECT: "Hand elegantly holding bottle, pouring liquid into glass, gentle splash"（完整瓶身倾倒必须有手）
- ✅ CORRECT: "Close-up of bottle neck pouring liquid, hand implied beyond frame"（局部倾倒可以不出现手）
- ✅ CORRECT: "Liquid splash frozen mid-air, ice cubes falling, [Product] bottle placed on surface"（飞溅特写，产品静置）
- ❌ WRONG: "Full bottle floating in air, pouring liquid"（完整瓶身悬空倾倒，违反物理）
- ❌ WRONG: "Bottle tilted pouring without support"（无支撑倾倒，不合理）

**真实感**：
- shot on [camera brand], professional camera
- not 3D render, not CGI, not overly perfect
- natural imperfections, organic placement
- authentic textures, real photography aesthetic
- photographic quality, genuine photoshoot look

**材质质感**：
- glass: crystal clear, transparent, reflective
- metal: metallic sheen, brushed finish, chrome
- ice: translucent, internal cracks, frost coating
- liquid: fluid, semi-transparent, light transmission

**构图**：
- rule of thirds, centered composition
- balanced arrangement, visual hierarchy
- depth layers: foreground, midground, background
- natural asymmetry, organic placement
