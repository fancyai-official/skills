## Template Usage Notes

- **Compact campaigns** (1–3 inputs): 6 images — 3 hero + 2 editorial + 1 campaign. **Full campaigns** (4+ inputs): 9 images — 5 hero + 2 editorial + 2 campaign. Omit hero 4–5 and campaign 2 rows/images for compact.
- **Embed images** using relative paths to the `outputs/` folder.
- **Captions**: Every image gets a one-line italic caption (outfit, model, setting).
- **Manifest**: Fill in the Look and Model columns to track which outfit and cast member appears in each shot.
